SChartiOS7Theme Class

The SChartiOS7Theme features solid-colored series without gradients on a light background and crisp and clear text to fit in with your iOS 7 app.

Syntax

[MonoTouch.Foundation.Register("SChartiOS7Theme", true)]
public class SChartiOS7Theme : SChartTheme

Remarks

See SChartTheme for an explanation of how themes work.
